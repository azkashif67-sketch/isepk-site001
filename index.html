Fire Detection & Alarm

Addressable smoke, heat and gas detection wired into a single panel, with early-warning routing to your team before a site-wide alarm triggers.

Zone-by-zone early warning

The panel identifies the exact detector that fired, so your team responds to a room, not a building.

Factory & warehouse rated detectors

Heat and gas sensing where dust, humidity or fumes would false-trigger ordinary smoke heads.

Civil Defence documentation

System drawings and test certificates prepared for building-approval and insurance inspections.

Voice evacuation & PA integration

Alarm events can trigger floor-specific announcements instead of one deafening siren.

CCTV Surveillance

High-resolution IP coverage with analytics that flag events as they happen — reviewed live from anywhere, or pulled from 90-day retention.

Analytics, not just recording

Remote viewing on mobile

Owners and managers watch live feeds and receive event clips on their phone — from any city.

Night & low-light performance
